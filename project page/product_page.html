About this area

Located in Panaji, Goa Marriott Resort & Spa is connected to a shopping center. Mapusa Friday Bazaar and Anjuna Flea Market are worth checking out if shopping is on the agenda, while those wishing to experience the area's natural beauty can explore Miramar Beach and Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary. Traveling with kids? Consider Goa Science Centre and Public Observatory. Practice your golf swing on a nearby course, or enjoy other activities in the great outdoors, such as ecotours in the area.
